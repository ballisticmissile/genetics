Welcome to Genix Labs Sales Dashboard

Explore detailed sales analytics for our genetic lab supplies, including DNA sequencing kits, PCR reagents, and gene-editing tools. Use these interactive dashboards to optimize your sales strategy and inventory planning.

Product Sales Performance

This dashboard provides a detailed view of sales performance across different product categories offered by Genix Labs, such as DNA sequencing kits, PCR reagents, and gene-editing tools. It features a bar chart that highlights top-selling products by revenue, allowing sales teams to identify which genetic supplies are driving growth. A line graph tracks monthly sales trends, helping to spot seasonal demand spikes—crucial for planning inventory of specialized lab equipment.

Regional Sales Distribution

Focused on regional sales distribution, this dashboard maps out how Genix Labs’ products are performing across various geographic markets. It includes a heat map showing sales density by region, with color gradients indicating high-demand areas like research hubs. A pie chart breaks down the contribution of each region to total sales, offering insights into where marketing efforts or new lab partnerships could be prioritized.

Sales Overview

This comprehensive dashboard combines key sales metrics into a single view, ideal for executive overviews. It features a stacked area chart displaying total sales over time, segmented by product type, to reveal how different genetic supplies contribute to revenue growth. Additionally, a KPI card highlights the year-to-date sales target achievement, while a table lists top-performing clients, aiding in relationship management with key lab institutions.
